Magic Cake

Magic Cake is one of the tastest cakes the world has ever know. Fex hane tried it and lived to tell about it, and these are their stories.

We also need to stress that whatever happens to you as a result of consuming Magic Cake is your responsibility. You've been warned, and you can view our legal page foe more information.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pound sugar
  • 3 sticks of butter

Direction

  1. Mix edds, sugar, and butter in a large bowl
  2. Spread into a non-stick dish
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour
  4. Let sit at room temperature for 20 minutes
  5. Eat and enjoy!
